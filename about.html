Welcome To The World Of Needle Magicians!

About us

With over 55 years of expertise in the clothing industry, NeedleMagix is the brainchild of the founders. It has been built on the vision of uncompromising quality and timeless design.

We started as a small business in Bangalore with big dreams. Our passion for composing exclusively customized menswear drives us to provide our customers with nothing but the highest quality of clothing that is guaranteed to make them stand out.

Our Mission

With a motivated and dedicated team, we strive to be the creative minds that bring a smile to your face. That’s why we’re always looking for innovative ways to give the best to you. We strive to make bespoke ethnic and formal menswear accessible to everyone.

Every piece in our collection is made to serve as your trusted companion through all walks of life!

Not Just Another Men’s Apparel Brand!

True Specialism & Expertise

When you choose NeedleMagix, you are choosing from a collection put together by the best designers. With the finest fabrics ranging from cotton, linen, Terry wool to made-to-measure clothing, you are assured of top quality and comfort.

100% Made-to-Measure

We are an apparel brand specializing in providing tailored perfection to you. Pick from our wide range of timeless ready-to-wear shirts or get your shirts, trousers, suits, or ethnic wear customised with the finest fabrics to get a perfect fit every time. We, at NeedleMagix, are all set to take you on this wonderful journey from fabric selection to sophisticated clothing.

Unique Styles

From quality fabrics to one-of-a-kind prints and stitches, NeedleMagix is truly committed to offering the most unique styles. Every piece of our collection is exclusively made to befit your unique personality and style dialect with the hope that you enjoy your clothing as much as we enjoy crafting it for you.

Consumer-Centricity

We understand the value of apparel in setting your individuality and chivalry. A truly congenial experience is what we aim to render to our consumers. Consumer-centricity is at the heart of NeedleMagix and each bit of customisation is a priority for us.
